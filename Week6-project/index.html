Dish of the Day

Featured Dishes

Suya

Spicy grilled meat skewers coated with ground peanuts and spices.

Vendor: Galaxy Grill, 12 Market Road, Lagos

Puff-Puff

Soft, deep-fried sweet dough balls dusted with sugar.

Vendor: Sweet Bites Corner, 45 Foodie Lane, Abuja

Akara

Golden black-eyed pea fritters, crispy outside and fluffy inside.

Vendor: Mama Nkechi’s Stall, 78 Osbourne Street, Port Harcourt

Boli

Roasted plantain served with spicy groundnut sauce.

Vendor: Boli Express, 22 Waterfront Drive, Calabar

Moi-Moi

Steamed bean pudding made from blended black-eyed peas and spices.

Vendor: Hand of God Canteen, 99 Freedom Avenue, Ibadan

Eggroll

Crispy pastry filled with egg, vegetables, and spices.

Vendor: Roll & Bite, 18 Unity Road, Enugu

Gizdodo

A flavorful combo of gizzard and fried plantain sautéed in spicy pepper sauce.

Vendor: Dodo Hub, 7 Tasty Street, Lagos

Fish Roll

Deep-fried pastry roll filled with seasoned fish mix, crunchy outside, savory inside.

Vendor: Crunchy Wraps, 10 Spice Junction, Uyo
