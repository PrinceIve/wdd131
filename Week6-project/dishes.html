Explore the Flavors of Nigeria

From Lagos to Port Harcourt, taste dishes that tell Naija’s story.

Suya

Spicy grilled meat skewers.

Vendor: Galaxy Grill, Lagos

Puff-Puff

Deep-fried dough balls, sweet and fluffy.

Vendor: Sweet Bites Corner, Abuja

Akara

Crispy black-eyed pea fritters.

Vendor: Mama Nkechi’s Stall, Port Harcourt

Boli

Charcoal-roasted ripe plantain, often served with pepper sauce or groundnuts.

Vendor: Boli Express, Calabar

Moi Moi

Steamed bean pudding wrapped in leaves, rich with peppers and fish or eggs.

Vendor: Hand of God Canteen, Ibadan

Egg Roll

Hard-boiled egg wrapped in slightly sweet dough and deep-fried to golden brown.

Vendor: Roll & Bite, Enugu

Nkwobi

A spicy cow foot delicacy prepared with palm oil and spices, often served in small bowls.

Vendor: Ugonma Kitchen, Owerri

Abacha

Cassava-based African salad mixed with palm oil, ugba, fish, and seasonings.

Vendor: Mama Chi’s Spot, Onitsha
